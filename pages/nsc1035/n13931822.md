  <ok href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E6%8B%BE%E8%8D%92%E8%80%85.html">

  </ok>

 </p>
 <p>
  原来，这位商人因为修炼法轮大法、心怀
  <ok href="https://www.epochtimes.com/gb/tag/%E5%96%84%E5%BF%B5.html">

  </ok>

 </p>
